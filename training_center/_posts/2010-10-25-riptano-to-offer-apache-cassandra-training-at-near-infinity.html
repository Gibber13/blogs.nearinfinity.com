The new NIC-U Training Center will host an Apache Cassandra training class on November 5th provided by Riptano.

This training session takes you from 0 to 60 buiding scalable applications on Apache Cassandra. This session is appropriate for developers and DBAs looking to understand design principles involved in modeling against Cassandra, as well as best practices for deploying and maintaining a Cassandra cluster.

To learn more and to register please visit the following link: http://www.eventbrite.com/event/900402127

The training provider, Riptano, is a consultancy that provides expert support and other services for Apache Cassandra, the leading scalable database for demanding workloads.
