Caroline Wizeman is Near Infinity's Director of Marketing and Communications and has been with the company since 2006. She manages NIC's brand, marketing and communications strategy, and public website as well as product marketing efforts for NIC Labs. She works to showcase the company's fun, tech-savvy culture in all of NIC's communications.

Prior to joining Near Infinity, Caroline worked for 10 years in marketing and communications at Lafarge North America and Winholesale Inc. Her focus has been on intranet/web design, online communications, creative direction, and graphic design. She graduated summa cum laude with a B.A. in Communications from the University of Dayton.
