Jeff joined Near Infinity in 2006 and is dedicated to building and maintaining a company that focuses on developing great software through the right mix of technology, talent, innovation and process. Jeff has spent the last ten years leading software development projects and coaching teams on Agile development techniques. While certain projects enjoyed tremendous success, others failed miserably. The sum of his experience has solidified his belief in Agile Methodologies. In his role at Near Infinity, Jeff helps our customers and employees transform their development organizations thereby returning value to their missions through the effective application of process.

Jeff began his career with American Management Systems working on a financial system and custom data warehouse web application for the Defense Advanced Research Projects Agency. He was exposed to Business Process Re-Engineering and web content management design when working for IBM Business Consulting Services. Prior to working for Near Infinity, Jeff led a development team at BAE Systems that was pioneering the use of J2EE within the Federal Government. While at Near Infinity, Jeff has coached organizations through the adoption of Agile development practices and currently leads a development team that is building an enterprise level search and retrieval system.

Jeff holds a B.S. in Commerce with concentrations in Management Information Systems and Finance from the University of Virginia. He is also a Certified Scrum Master.
